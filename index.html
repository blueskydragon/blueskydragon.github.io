Hand Forms

Animal Style

Snake

The Snake style adopts the flexibility and fluidity of a snake which allows them to defend themselves better and their attacks would be more unexpected which will give them an advantage. Snake style is under the “softer” side of Martial Arts.

Monkey

The Monkey style is one of the 5 animals in Kung Fu. this style is like the movements of an ape or monkey. This style has the looks of jumping and crawling around like a Monkey.

Southern Style

Baji

Baji, Bajiquan, or Kaimen ba Ji Quan is a type of hand form that is explosive, has short-range power and is famous for its elbow and shoulder strikes.

Southern Fist

Southern Fist or Nanquan is a form showing low stable stances and short powerful movements that combine both attack and defense. During practice, Southern fist uses full-body or arm techniques then acrobatics.

Northern Style

Bagua

Bagua, Baguazhang or Pakua chang is one of the three main Chinese martial arts of the Wudang school. Bagua means “eight trigram palm”, referring to the trigrams of I Ching (Yijing).

Long Fist

Long Fist or Changquan is a style from the northern part of China. Long fist has full extended strikes like kicks and punches. Long fist has a motto "the best defense is a strong offense," which means that the attack of Long fist is so hard the opponent won't have any time to attack back.

Weapons

Long Weapons

Staff

Staff also called a Gun is a long bamboo stick used in many types of martial arts. It is known as one of the four major weapons and is the "The Grandfather of all Weapons". It has a thin base at the top and at the bottom, it has a thicker base.

Spear

Spear also called a Qiang is a long bamboo stick with a sharp metal at the top and is used in many types of martial arts. It is known as one of the four major weapons and is the "The King of Weapons". The bamboo is more flexible than a normal bamboo stick. The sharp metal part has a ball that makes sound and it has a tassel.

Short Weapons

Broad Sword

Broad Sword also called a Dao is a piece of flimsy metal that is wider than normal blades. It is known as one of the four major weapons and is the "The General of Weapons". It is used for slicing and chopping. The sword has a piece of fabric attached to the bottom of it.

Straight Sword

Straight Sword also called a Jian is a piece of flimsy metal that is thin and straight. It is known as one of the four major weapons and is the "The Gentleman of Weapons". Jian can also be used as a taichi sword. Some of the swords would have a bone in the middle of the sword.

Other Weapons

Fan

The Tai Chi Taiji Kung Fu fighting fan or the Iron Fan is a weapon that was used in the old day in china. Iron fans are usually used ceremonial but if needed would be used for combat. It would be used to assassinate a target.

Whip Chain

The Whip chain or also known as the soft whip is made of metal rods, which are connected by rings to make it flexible. The whip has a metal dart on one end and a handle on the other.
